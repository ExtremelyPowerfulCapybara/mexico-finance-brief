Nasdaq Drops 1.7–2% as Nvidia Earnings Fail to Impress; AI Bubble Fears Resurface
Nvidia posted strong quarterly results but its stock still fell roughly 5%, dragging the Nasdaq down nearly 2% and the S&P 500 over 1% — a textbook 'sell the news' moment after a 40% AI-sector rally over six months. Rising bond yield concerns and inflation uncertainty compounded the pressure. The FTSE 100, by contrast, hit a record high on Rolls-Royce's 40% profit jump, underlining the divergence between stretched US tech and recovering European industrials.

Dogecoin Foundation Eyes Real-World Asset Tokenization as Path to Institutional Utility
Dogecoin Foundation director Timothy Stebbing has outlined a two-to-three year plan to position DOGE as the trading currency for tokenized real-world assets — hotels, minerals, oil and gas — via a sidechain called Fractal Engine, with eventual migration to the base layer. The pitch lands squarely in the middle of a tokenization narrative being driven at the institutional level by BlackRock, whose CEO Larry Fink argued in his 2025 chairman's letter that every asset class could eventually be tokenized. Whether a meme coin can credibly anchor that infrastructure remains, to put it charitably, an open question.

CBC News Politics
Canada Softens Stance on India Foreign Interference Ahead of Carney Trade Trip
A senior Canadian government official told reporters that Ottawa is now confident India is no longer actively interfering in Canadian democratic processes — a sharp reversal from 2024, when RCMP Commissioner Mike Duheme cited evidence of the 'highest levels' of the Indian government orchestrating violence and intimidation on Canadian soil. Public Safety Minister Gary Anandasangaree stopped short of endorsing that characterization, saying 'there's a lot more work to do.' The pivot comes as Prime Minister Mark Carney pursues economic normalization with New Delhi, a calculation that has geopolitical and trade implications that extend well beyond bilateral relations.

Starknet Launches strkBTC: Privacy-Focused Bitcoin Asset for DeFi, as STRK Token Down 70% in 90 Days
Starknet is preparing to launch strkBTC, a shielded Bitcoin asset for its Layer-2 network that allows users to transact BTC in DeFi without exposing balances or counterparties, with selective disclosure mechanisms designed to preserve regulatory compliance. The launch is aimed at attracting institutional participation that has historically been deterred by on-chain transparency. Native token STRK sits at roughly $0.042, down approximately 70% over the past 90 days, making the strkBTC catalyst a high-stakes test of whether product development can outrun token price deterioration.

Financial Post / GlobeNewswire Markets
Ninepoint Partners Closes First Tranche of 2026 Flow-Through LP at $81.25 Million
Toronto-based alternative asset manager Ninepoint Partners has completed the first closing of its 2026 Flow-Through Limited Partnership, raising $81.25 million across 3.25 million units at $25.00 each, with a second closing targeted for March 19. The partnership offers investors approximately 100% tax deductibility on invested capital, with a planned rollover to Ninepoint's Resource Fund class between January and February 2028. The offering is distributed through a syndicate led by RBC Dominion Securities and includes most of Canada's major dealer networks.

Foster + Partners Reveals Updated Design for Two World Trade Center, American Express HQ
Foster + Partners and Silverstein Properties have released new renderings of Two World Trade Center, a 1,226-foot, 55-storey supertall that will serve as the sole headquarters for American Express and house up to 10,000 workers across nearly two million square feet. Construction is set to begin in spring 2026 with completion targeted for 2031, completing a development that has been under construction for nearly two decades.
