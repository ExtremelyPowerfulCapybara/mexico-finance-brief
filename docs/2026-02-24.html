Marca / BusinessLine Mexico
El Mencho killed in Jalisco raid, triggering cartel retaliation and US shelter-in-place warnings across Mexico
Mexican special forces, with US intelligence support, killed CJNG leader Nemesio 'El Mencho' Oseguera Cervantes, 59, in a raid in Tapalpa, Jalisco on Sunday. The operation — for which the US had offered up to $15 million — sparked immediate cartel retaliation: vehicle torchings, roadblocks, and armed clashes across Jalisco, Tamaulipas, Guanajuato, and Michoacán. The US State Department issued shelter-in-place warnings for Americans in Puerto Vallarta and Guadalajara, while United, Alaska, Southwest, and Air Canada canceled or diverted flights to both cities.
