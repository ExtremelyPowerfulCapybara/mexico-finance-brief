Supreme Court Strikes Down Trump's Sweeping Tariffs, But Markets Remain Uneasy
U.S. equity futures fell Monday — S&P 500 -0.5%, Dow -0.6%, Nasdaq -0.7% — even after the Supreme Court voided most of Trump's broad tariff package late last week. The relief was short-lived: Trump immediately announced a 10-15% global tariff via executive order and signaled further Commerce Department-led levies, keeping trade policy as 'a movable feast,' per Rabobank's Benjamin Picton. For Mexico, the ruling offers a narrow reprieve, but Trump's pivot to alternative mechanisms means the threat to export-dependent sectors isn't gone.

Marca Mexico
El Mencho Killed, CJNG Violence Erupts in Jalisco — FIFA Weighs Guadalajara's World Cup Role
Mexican security forces killed Nemesio Oseguera Cervantes 'El Mencho,' leader of the Jalisco New Generation Cartel, triggering widespread reprisals including burning roadblocks, arson, and flight cancellations around Guadalajara and Puerto Vallarta. Burnt vehicles lined the highway near Akron Stadium, Liga MX matches were postponed, and authorities declared a 'red alert' across parts of the metro area. FIFA has not publicly pulled Guadalajara's hosting rights, but sources say concerns are high with four group-stage matches, two playoff games, and the South Korean and Colombian national team base camps already committed to the city.

Five Crypto Exchanges Allegedly Helping Russia Evade Sanctions Post-Garantex Takedown
Blockchain analytics firm Elliptic identified five exchanges — Bitpapa, ABCeX, Exmo, Rapira, and Aifory Pro — as filling the vacuum left by sanctioned Russian exchange Garantex, which was shut down in March 2025. ABCeX reportedly processed at least $11 billion in crypto from Moscow's Federation Tower, the same address Garantex used, while Exmo.com and Exmo.me allegedly share custodial wallet infrastructure with over $19.5 million in direct transactions with sanctioned entities. The NSE's latest report shows new investor registrations in India hit 17.7 lakh in January 2026, but the pace of growth has clearly decelerated: the most recent 1 crore addition took eight months versus five to six months for prior milestones, with the exchange citing tariff-related global headwinds as the primary cause.
